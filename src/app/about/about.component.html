About Us

We are the South East Stars. A little league farm-level girls softball team out of the Mount Tabor neighborhood.

We care about fundamentals, teamwork, and having fun.

We don't sweat the small stuff. We shake it off like Taylor Swift when we goof up.

We love getting outs, hitting the ball, running bases hard head down, and encouraging each other to take another swing at the ball

We play to win but don't rub it in.

We are the Southeast Stars.
