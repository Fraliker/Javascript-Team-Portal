About Us

We are the South East Stars. A farm league girls softball team out of the Mount Tabor neighborhood.

We care about fundamentals, teamwork, and having fun.

We don't sweat the small stuff. We shake it off like Taylor Swift when we goof up.

We love getting outs,

gitting hits,

and encouraging each other to give it another swing.

We play to win but know how to lose.

We don't rub it in.

We are the Southeast Stars.
